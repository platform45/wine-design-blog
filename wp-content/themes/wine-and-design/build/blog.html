Alex Latimer

Alex Latimer is a writer and illustrator based in Cape Town, from whom we heard an inspiring talk at Wine & Design in Cape Town. His picture books for children (The Boy Who Cried Ninja, Penguin’s Hidden Talent and Lion vs Rabbit) have been translated and published all over the world. Locally he has created The South African Alphabet, From Aardvark to Zuma and has illustrated Rudyard Kipling’s Just So Stories all for Penguin South Africa. Alex was also half of the team that created the cartoon The Western Nostril that was published four days a week in the Business Day newspaper for three years. Two books of The Western Nostril exist, but are almost impossible to find. His first novel for adults, The Space Race, will be published by Umuzi and will be released in September this year.

For more info on Alex Latimer, visit: http://www.alexlatimer.co.za
